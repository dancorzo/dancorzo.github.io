Who I am

I graduated from Tec de Monterrey in 2013 with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. I kickstarted my career as a Process Engineer developing manufacturing and client success practices for companies like Federal Mogul, Gerdau, and Tenaris-Tamsa.

My interest in Solar Energy & Tech Development led me to Saudi Arabia, where I obtained my masters and PhD in Materials Science from King Abdullah University of Science and technology (KAUST). Here, I managed projects for scalable manufacturing of solar cells, capacitors, batteries, & sensors through printing technologies like inkjet printing, screen Printing, slot-die coating, direct ink writing, and 3D printing.

I enjoy doing applied research and making functional materials sustainable, scalable, and accesible for new applications in medicine, sports, AR/VR, and IoT. I also love finding trends in data to develop successful implementation strategies.

When I'm not doing research, I focus on doing science communication, producing electronic music, painting, and learning new languages.

Projects

Inkjet Printed Solar Cells

Developed Inks of Photoactive Layer, Transport Layers, and Electrodes to produce Freeform, Lightweight, Semitransparent, and Flexible OPV with efficiencies of up to 12.5%.

Slot Die Coated Solar Modules

Optimized wetting conditions through ink formulation and surface modification to produce large area solar modules (>12cm2 ) with high efficiencies.

Renewable Inks for Organic Electronics

Analyzed solubility parameters of organic molecules (OPV, OLEDs, OTFTs and OTEGs) to find green solvent alternatives with low toxicity and reduced carbon footprint.

Inkjet Printed Biosensors

Formulated water-proof inks and functionalized them with enzymes to detect analytes inlcuding glucose, lactose, dopamine, uric acid, and ascorbic acid.

Printed Supercapacitors & Batteries

Designed flexible solid-state supercapacitors with high stability over 10,000 bending cycles. The electrodes and ionic electrolytes were inkjet printed.

3D Printed E-skin

Developed a system for in-situ curing of 2-part resins and rubber silicones. Integrated functional nanoparticles to create condictive networks, and developed highly sensitive strain & pressure sensors for robotic prostheses & AR/VR applications.
